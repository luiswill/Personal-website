Unique experience

After completing my three-year bachelor in France/Germany, I had the chance to spend a two-month trip across some Southeast Asia countries, especially Thailand and Philippines.

I learned how other cultures, often poorer than my own country, live in their daily life. Meeting new people and discovering uncommon places teached me a lot of self-consciousness and ways of enjoying life.

The month in Thailand was hot, party and beach.

Philippines one month later in november 2018 was really cool, I worked as a volunteer (via workaway.info) in a small hostel to manage guests and meals. I helped the hostel owner with everything and she offered me her hospitality with her delicious meals. We had pets in the garden, a lot of cats and two dogs. She learned me a lot of gardening, cooking, managing and cultural knowledge. She was really nice and helpful. Thank you Kakay
